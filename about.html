Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, moral integrity, and social responsibility. We aim to empower our students to become compassionate, confident, and competent individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be an institution that inspires a lifelong love for learning, cultivates a spirit of inquiry, and upholds the principles of justice, peace, and equality.

Our Legacy

For over 170 years, Modern National School has stood as a pillar of education in Patna. Our legacy is built on a foundation of faith, service, and community. We take pride in our tradition of academic rigor, character building, and a deep sense of social responsibility. Our alumni, who have excelled in various fields, continue to carry forward the values instilled during their time here.

Holistic Education

We believe that education goes beyond textbooks. Our curriculum is designed to challenge students intellectually, foster creativity, and encourage critical thinking. In addition to academics, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, from sports to the arts, to ensure that our students grow in all dimensions—intellectual, emotional, physical, and spiritual.

Dedicated Faculty

Our experienced and passionate faculty are the heart of our institution. They are committed to nurturing each student’s unique talents and helping them achieve their full potential. With a student-centered approach, our teachers provide personalized guidance, ensuring that every student is supported on their educational journey.

Join Our Journey

We invite you to be a part of our vibrant community. Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or alumni, we welcome you to explore our school and discover what makes Demo National School a truly special place to learn and grow.
